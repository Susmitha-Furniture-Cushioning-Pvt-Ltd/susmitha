We've been Making The Furnitures Since 1993

Mr. Seelamsetty Raja & Seelamsetty Kishore, inspired by his father started crafting in their early childhood. Their passion for creative designs were beyond imagination. This artist gave The Furno a collection of Masterpieces that became Consumers' Love.

Since 2005, we are a furniture franchise brand that brings to you the most comfortable and creative fits for your house. Custom Creations are the main motto for the company, keeping in mind that people buy what they love. And we want people to love what they buy. Thanks for having us.
